Soyoung Yang

        

I am a PhD student at KAIST AI advised by Jaegul Choo.

My research interests lie in natural language processing (NLP), especially for information extraction (IE) that aims to extract structured information from unstructured text. I also interested in diverse applications in NLP and computer vision.

Before joining KAIST, I completed my Bachelor's degree at Korea University, majoring in Sinographic Literatures and Language, Brain & Computer as a relational major.
